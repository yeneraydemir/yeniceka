Why Are Hygiene Barriers Critical During Eid?

November 27, 2025

During Eid, higher staff counts and workload make hygiene control at entries critical. Without a turnstile, handwashing and knife sterilization, contamination risk rises fast. Entry barriers are the first line of defense to protect hygiene equipment and deboning line flow.

Entry Layout

  • Place handwashing and sole disinfection ahead of the turnstile.
  • Position knife sterilizers at the start of each line branch to avoid backtracking.
  • Use barriers with two-way control during peak hours.

Maintenance and Inspection

Each barrier needs a daily checklist: temperature, detergent/disinfectant levels and mechanical lock tests. Verify disinfectant levels at the start of every shift.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum setup for a hygiene barrier?

A handwashing sink, turnstile, sole disinfection and a knife sterilizer are mandatory at entry.

How do we manage higher traffic?

Add a dual-direction turnstile and extra handwashing points; guide staff at the line entry.

How often should checks be done?

Daily cleaning and temperature checks, weekly mechanical lock tests, monthly disinfectant and stainless surface inspections.
