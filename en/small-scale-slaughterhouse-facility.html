Small-Scale Slaughterhouse

Less space, lower investment, high hygiene standards

A small-scale slaughterhouse is the most practical way to prevent illegal slaughter, secure local meat supply and comply with food regulations — all within a limited budget and space.

These facilities, designed for municipal abattoirs, village cooperatives and small meat businesses, meet the same hygiene and inspection standards as large slaughterhouses while occupying much less space and being commissioned much faster.

At Ceka Abattoir we manage site measurement, layout planning, equipment selection, manufacturing and installation end-to-end. We include operator training and a maintenance plan at handover, so you're operational, inspectable and sustainable from day one.
